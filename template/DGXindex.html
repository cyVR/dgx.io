Purchase & Store Gold

Buy only LBMA approved Pamp Suisse gold bars from a DigixGlobal. Certifications of proof are always provided. All customers' assets are segregated from Digixglobal's assets. Digixglobal has zero rights to them.

PoA™ Smart Contracts

Proof of Asset (PoA™) is a tri-party digital certificate and agreement among the supplier of gold, the certified custodian and an independent auditor to create DGX Gold vouchers on the Ethereum blockchain.

Redeeming DGX Gold

DGX Gold vouchers can be converted into physical gold bars for vault storage, shipment, or physical pick up. For ease of liquidation, they can also be exchanged for cyrpto currencies and assets on EtherEx.
